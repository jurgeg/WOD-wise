WODwise Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

WODwise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Password: Securely hashed and stored for authentication

Fitness Profile Data

  • Experience level: Your CrossFit experience (beginner, intermediate, advanced, competitor)
  • Movement skills: Self-reported skill ratings for various exercises
  • Strength numbers: Your 1-rep max records for major lifts
  • Limitations: Any injuries, equipment constraints, or movement restrictions

Workout Data

  • Workout photos: Images of gym whiteboards you photograph for analysis
  • Parsed workouts: Structured workout data extracted from your photos
  • Workout history: Past workouts and strategies for your reference

How We Use Your Information

  • Workout analysis: We use AI to parse workout images and generate personalized strategies
  • Personalization: Your fitness profile helps us tailor scaling recommendations and pacing strategies
  • Service improvement: Anonymous usage data helps us improve the app experience

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate WODwise:

  • Supabase: Secure database and authentication services (data stored in the United States)
  • Anthropic Claude: AI service for workout image analysis and strategy generation

Workout photos are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. These images are processed in real-time and are not stored by Anthropic beyond the immediate processing request.

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Fitness profile: Retained until you delete your account
  • Workout photos: Processed in-memory for analysis; not permanently stored on our servers
  • Workout history: Retained until you delete your account or individual entries

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted via HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device
  • Database access is protected by row-level security policies

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account
  • Correction: Update your fitness profile at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

WODwise is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
